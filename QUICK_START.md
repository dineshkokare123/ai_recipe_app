## ğŸ’¡ Tips & Tricks

### For Best AI Results:
- Be specific in your prompts
- Example: "Spicy Thai chicken with vegetables" instead of just "chicken"
- Include cooking method if desired: "grilled", "baked", "stir-fried"

- First load might take a moment to initialize database
- Subsequent loads are instant
- Images are cached for faster loading

## ğŸ› Troubleshooting

### "Failed to generate recipe"
- Check your internet connection
- Verify your API key is correct
- Ensure you haven't exceeded API limits (60/min, 1500/day)

### App crashes on startup
- Run `flutter clean` then `flutter pub get`
- Rebuild the app

### No recipes showing
- Check if you're on the right tab
- Clear any active filters
- Try generating a new recipe
